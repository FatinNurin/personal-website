1. Influenza Infection

This is my first (and hopefully last) time being infected with Influenza B. This infection struck me while I was studying on campus. At first, I just felt ill and
continued to attend the interim programme. I began to feel dizzy and on the verge of fainting while walking down the hall. As a result, I decided
to go to the Health Unit. And at the time, I was only diagnosed with a fever and low blood pressure. The doctor issued me with a Medical Certificate
(MC) that allowed me to leave the interim programme earlier. My fever worsened after I returned home, my parents took me to the hospital for
hospital for further treatment. And it was only after my blood test results that I was diagnosed with Influenza B infection.
